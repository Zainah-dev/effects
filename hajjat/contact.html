Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements?

Our programs are open to anyone with a passion for learning tech skills. While some advanced courses may require prerequisite knowledge, most of our beginner programs have no technical requirements. You'll need a computer and reliable internet connection.

Do you offer financial aid or payment plans?

Yes! We offer several payment options including monthly installments and income share agreements for eligible students. We also partner with organizations that provide scholarships for underrepresented groups in tech.

How does the job placement support work?

Our career services team provides resume reviews, interview preparation, portfolio building, and networking opportunities. We also have partnerships with tech companies who hire directly from our graduate pool. 85% of our graduates land tech jobs within 6 months of completing their program.

Can I take courses part-time while working?

Absolutely! We offer flexible scheduling options including evening and weekend classes. Many of our students successfully balance our programs with full-time jobs or other commitments.
