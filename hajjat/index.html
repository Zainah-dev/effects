About BreakThrough Tech Academy

Founded in 2020, BreakThrough Tech Academy was created to bridge the gap between traditional education and the rapidly evolving tech industry. Our programs are designed by industry experts to give students the practical skills employers are looking for.

We believe that anyone can learn to code and succeed in tech, regardless of their background. Our inclusive learning environment supports students at every step of their journey.

  • Industry-relevant curriculum
  • Project-based learning
  • Career support and mentorship
  • Flexible learning options
